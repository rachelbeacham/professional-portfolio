Customer Service => Web Developer

Hello! My name is Rachel, I am a Web Developer with a passion for building exciting projects in code. My strongest technologies include React, JavaScript, CSS3, and HTML5.

While earning my Bachelors degree from UCSB and after graduating I attained 5 years of professional experience in the Customer Service industry. The most fulfilling part of working in customer service was using creativity and problem solving skills to find the best possible solution for both the customer and the business!

Early in 2020 a friend mentioned to me that she used those same skills in her role as a Web Developer. I spent a few months researching the industry and teaching myself the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and I fell in love with discovering how the apps, softwares, and web pages I use every day are actually made!

I attended an intensive Full Stack Web Development program at LearningFuze where I got to code all day every day and the concepts I've learned and applied have grown exponentially! I'm excited to continue learning and bring my skills to the table in a professional development role!
